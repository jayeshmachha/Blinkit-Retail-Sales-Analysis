🏪 Blinkit Retail Sales Analysis & Prediction Using Machine Learning
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail supermarket sales data to identify key factors affecting product and outlet performance. The objective is to generate business insights and build a machine learning model capable of predicting future sales to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Business Objective

The retail company aims to:

Improve product sales performance

Optimize inventory management

Analyze outlet performance

Understand customer purchasing behavior

Support outlet expansion strategy

🚨 Business Problems Solved

Identification of low-performing products

Comparison of outlet performance across locations

Analysis of product visibility impact on sales

Inventory optimization recommendations

Customer preference analysis (Low Fat vs Regular products)

Relationship between product ratings and sales

📊 Dataset Information

The dataset contains information related to:

Product categories

Outlet types and sizes

Store locations

Product visibility

Customer ratings

Sales performance

🤖 Machine Learning Model

Model Used: Random Forest Regressor

Model Performance

✅ R² Score: 0.93

✅ Mean Absolute Error: 4.48

The model successfully predicts product sales with high accuracy.

📈 Key Insights

Outlet size significantly impacts total sales

Product visibility influences purchasing behavior

Certain product categories drive major revenue

Medium-sized outlets show strong performance

Sales trends can be predicted using historical data

This project demonstrates an end-to-end Blinkit retail sales analytics workflow, transforming raw business data into actionable insights and predictive intelligence. The developed machine learning model enables accurate sales forecasting and supports strategic decision-making for inventory optimization, outlet performance improvement, and future business expansion.
